How to Play

  1. Connect to the same WiFi or internet at home.
  2. One player clicks Host, the other clicks Join.
  3. Copy the signal from Your Signal and send it to your friend.
  4. Your friend pastes it into Peer-Signal, and sends back their signal.
  5. You paste their signal into Peer-Signal.
  6. Both click Submit Signal to connect.
  7. Start playing. Host is X, Join is O.
  8. Click Disconnect to end the game.
You can keep this panel open while playing or close it anytime.
