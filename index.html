How to Play

  1. Connect to the same WiFi or internet at home.
  2. One of you clicks the first button at the top (it says Host), and the other clicks the second button (it says Join).
  3. The first player will see a box called Your Signal. Copy everything in that box and send it to your friend (for example, using chat or email).
  4. Your friend should paste what you sent into the box called Peer-Signal and copy their own Your Signal box to send back to you.
  5. Paste what your friend sent you into your Peer-Signal box.
  6. Both of you click the green Submit Signal button.
  7. Now you can play TicTacToe together! The first player is X, the second player is O.
  8. If you want to stop playing, click the red Disconnect button.
You can keep this panel open while playing, or close it anytime.
